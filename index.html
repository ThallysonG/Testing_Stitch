Master Artist Spotlight

Dr. Woo

Brian Woo, known professionally as Dr. Woo, has redefined the single-needle aesthetic. His intricate, mathematical designs and celestial mappings have turned the tattoo into a high-fashion accessory and a deeply personal fine art.

Based in Los Angeles at his private suite, Woo's work is characterized by "fine line" precision—ghostly, delicate patterns that seem to float on the skin rather than be carved into it. He has inked icons across the globe, bridging the gap between underground culture and global luxury.

The Ritual of Healing

Aftercare is not a suggestion; it is the final stage of the artistic process.

  • Cleanse twice daily with saline solution
  • Avoid pools, saunas, and direct sun for 14 days
  • Do not twist, turn, or touch the jewelry
  • Wear loose-fitting, breathable natural fabrics
